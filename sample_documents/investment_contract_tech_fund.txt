INVESTMENT MANAGEMENT AGREEMENT
Technology Growth Fund L.P.

Date: December 1, 2023
Parties: ACME Financial Corporation (Investment Manager) and Technology Growth Fund L.P. (Fund)

ARTICLE 1 - APPOINTMENT AND DUTIES

1.1 Investment Manager Appointment
The Fund hereby appoints ACME Financial Corporation as the investment manager to provide discretionary investment management services for the Fund's portfolio.

1.2 Investment Objectives
The Fund seeks long-term capital appreciation by investing primarily in equity securities of technology companies with market capitalizations exceeding $1 billion.

1.3 Investment Guidelines
- Minimum 80% allocation to technology sector securities
- Maximum 5% position size in any single security
- Maximum 20% allocation to international securities
- No investments in private securities or derivatives
- Cash reserves maintained at 2-10% of portfolio value

ARTICLE 2 - COMPENSATION AND FEES

2.1 Management Fee
The Investment Manager shall receive an annual management fee equal to 1.25% of the Fund's average net asset value, calculated and payable quarterly.

2.2 Performance Fee
The Investment Manager shall receive a performance fee equal to 20% of net profits above the high-water mark, subject to a 6% hurdle rate based on the Russell 1000 Technology Index.

2.3 Fee Calculation
Management fees are calculated on the last business day of each quarter based on average daily net asset value. Performance fees are calculated annually.

ARTICLE 3 - TERM AND TERMINATION

3.1 Initial Term
This Agreement shall have an initial term of five (5) years commencing December 1, 2023.

3.2 Renewal Terms
The Agreement may be renewed for successive one-year periods by mutual written consent.

3.3 Termination Rights
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 90 days written notice. The Fund may terminate immediately for cause.

ARTICLE 4 - FUND OPERATIONS

4.1 Net Asset Value
NAV calculations performed daily using third-party pricing services. Month-end valuations subject to independent audit.

4.2 Reporting Requirements
Monthly performance reports due within 15 business days. Quarterly reports include detailed holdings and attribution analysis.

4.3 Compliance Monitoring
Investment Manager maintains compliance with all investment guidelines and regulatory requirements.

ARTICLE 5 - REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

5.1 Investment Manager Representations
- Registered investment adviser under Investment Advisers Act of 1940
- Maintains professional liability insurance of $50 million
- No material conflicts of interest
- Adequate systems and controls for fund management

5.2 Fund Representations
- Validly organized limited partnership
- Authority to enter into this Agreement
- Will provide necessary documentation for regulatory compliance

ARTICLE 6 - INDEMNIFICATION

6.1 Limitation of Liability
Investment Manager liability limited to gross negligence or willful misconduct. Not liable for market losses or force majeure events.

6.2 Indemnification
Fund agrees to indemnify Investment Manager against third-party claims arising from authorized investment activities.

ARTICLE 7 - CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of proprietary information and comply with applicable privacy regulations.

ARTICLE 8 - GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of New York State. Disputes subject to binding arbitration.

ARTICLE 9 - FUND DETAILS

Fund Information:
- Fund Name: Technology Growth Fund L.P.
- Initial Capital: $250 million
- Target Size: $500 million
- Investment Period: 36 months
- Fund Life: 7 years with two 1-year extensions

Benchmark Performance:
- Primary Benchmark: Russell 1000 Technology Index
- Peer Comparison: Morningstar Technology Fund Category
- Risk Metrics: Maximum tracking error of 8%
